# Stress and Rest

Stress represents physical injury and hurt morale, with each character taking up to a maximum of ten stress before being out. If youâ€™re out, your character is still alive but cannot attempt or succeed at any rolls until recovered through rest. You cannot recover the stress of a character who is out through boons.

If a character rests for a considerable period of time (8-24 hours) in a non-stressful and safe environment, they rest. When you rest, clear all your stress and negative conditions and recover all your traits.
