    Tags

    Tags #

    Tags are narrative details about your character, each with a level from 1✦ to 5✦, that can provide a bonus to your rolls. You can only add one tag to a roll.

    For example, tags can represent any of the following:

    • Tags can represent who your character is and what forms their identity. For example, Scientist, Elf, Gigantic, Stubborn, Head of the Council, and Good Dad.

    • Tags can represent external items or allies your character has access to. For example, Pet Cat, Assault Rifle, Full-Plate Armor, Haunted RV, Loyal Butler, and Back-Alley Hideout.

    • Tags can represent special powers or moves your character can use. For example, Fireball, Deduction, Telekinesis, Necromancy, Wrestling, and Biology.

    Once you exhaust a tag, you cannot benefit from its bonus again until it’s recovered. You can recover tags through boons or rest.

    A tag being exhausted does not remove its presence in your character or the narrative. For example, if you have the tag Teleportation and exhaust it, you can still teleport in the story while it is exhausted. You just can’t benefit from the Teleportation tag when rolling to do so.
